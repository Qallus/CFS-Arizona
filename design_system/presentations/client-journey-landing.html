Client Meeting Journey

A plan built around your life.

Seven stages covering every dimension of your financial life — from your first conversation to your lasting legacy.

01
Complimentary Consultation & Alignment Complimentary

Initial Consultation & Discovery

Complimentary

A first conversation to understand your goals, questions, and overall financial picture. We may offer secure eMoney vault access so you can optionally share documents before we meet.

Vision Alignment Meeting

Optional

A second conversation, when helpful, to include other decision-makers, clarify goals, answer questions, and determine mutual fit.

02
Client Engagement & Account Setup
CE

Client Engagement & Account Setup

When both your family and SIG see strong alignment, your Client Service Associate guides agreements, eMoney setup, document collection, account openings/transfers, and the Risk Profile Questionnaire. Virtual or in person.

03
Financial Plan & Strategy

Personalized Financial Plan & Strategy Review

Your planner reviews your financial plan, key opportunities, risks, recommendations, and initial strategy.

Strategy & Implementation Follow-Up

As Needed

A deeper session to refine investment, tax, estate, income, or protection strategies before or during implementation.

90-Day Progress Review

Review early progress, completed items, next priorities, and confirm the relationship is moving forward well.

04
Ongoing Planning & Optimization

Financial Planning Update

Refresh the plan around life changes, goals, family priorities, and new opportunities.

Financial Planning Assumptions Review

Collaboratively review the assumptions driving your plan — retirement age, spending, inflation, longevity, Social Security, and more.

Investment / Portfolio Review

Review portfolio performance, allocation, market conditions, and alignment with your goals.

Tax Planning Meeting

Identify proactive tax strategies and coordinate with your CPA when appropriate.

Risk & Protection Review

Evaluate insurance, liability, and other protection gaps so your family remains protected.

Stress Testing / Scenario Planning

Model market, retirement, income, or life-event scenarios to test plan resilience.

05
Purpose & Legacy Distinctive to SIG

Family Investment Policy Statement

Create your family's financial playbook: goals, priorities, investment philosophy, and decision-making guardrails.

Stewardship Blueprint

Vision & Values

Clarify values, purpose, and the long-term vision for your life and family.

Stewardship Blueprint

Wealth & Impact

Align wealth with giving, impact, stewardship, and opportunities to bless others.

Stewardship Blueprint

Legacy & Family

Shape legacy, family communication, and generational impact.

06
Life Events & Specialized Planning

Life Event / Major Decision Meeting

Guidance around major transitions such as retirement, selling a business, loss of a loved one, or family meetings with children and heirs.

Philanthropic / Charitable Planning Meeting

Design charitable strategies that align generosity, values, legacy, and tax efficiency.

07
Annual Review
★

Annual Stewardship Review

A comprehensive annual review of your financial life, progress, priorities, and next steps. The capstone meeting of every planning year.
