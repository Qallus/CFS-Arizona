Client Meeting Journey · Stage 04
Ongoing Planning
04 · Ongoing Planning & Optimization

Your plan, continuously refined.

Six recurring meeting types that keep your financial life coordinated and current.

FU
Financial Planning Update

Refresh the plan around life changes, goals, family priorities, and new opportunities.

AR
Financial Planning Assumptions Review

Collaboratively review the assumptions driving your plan — retirement age, spending, inflation, longevity, Social Security, and more.

IP
Investment / Portfolio Review

Review portfolio performance, allocation, market conditions, and alignment with your goals.

TP
Tax Planning Meeting

Identify proactive tax strategies and coordinate with your CPA when appropriate.

RP
Risk & Protection Review

Evaluate insurance, liability, and other protection gaps so your family remains protected.

SS
Stress Testing / Scenario Planning

Model market, retirement, income, or life-event scenarios to test plan resilience.

Planner Note

These six meeting types recur as your life and plan evolve. Frequency is tailored to your planning tier and current priorities.
